// features

things it does, roughly in order of impressiveness

▶

Vertical scroll feed

Momentum physics, snap-to-video, the whole deal. Your wrist will hate you.

♪

Audio playback

Real audio via ffplay. Your coworkers will definitely think you're working.

⚡

YouTube & TikTok

Streams live. No account needed for YouTube. TikTok requires a stolen cookie.

📁

Local folders

Point it at a folder of videos. Offline doom-scrolling. A true innovation.

// usage

it's basically just termtok with flags

termtok # trending YouTube Shorts (the default)
termtok --search cats # search YouTube Shorts
termtok --user MrBeast # a channel's Shorts
termtok --tag funny # by hashtag
termtok -p tiktok # TikTok For-You feed (needs ms_token)
termtok /path/to/clips # local folder — fully offline
